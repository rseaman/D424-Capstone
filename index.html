VaycayTrak

Spreadsheets are for work, not vacations

My WGU Capstone project! VaycayTrak is an Android (SDK 26+) application that assists users with vacation planning.

The Problem: You've got flight confirmations in your email, hotel info in a screenshot, excursion bookings in three different apps, and that one restaurant recommendation your friend texted you at 2am.
The Solution: VaycayTrak keeps it all in one place. On your phone. Offline. No ads, no subscription. Just your vacation data, organized.

What it does:

  • Manage multiple vacation entries (dates, hotel info, the basics)
  • Add excursions and categorize them however makes sense to you
  • Search through everything when you can't remember which trip that one thing was for
  • Generate reports if you're into that (or if you need to expense stuff)
